What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a method that combines fish farming and plant growth in one system.

Plants Suitable for Aquaponics

Lettuce: Grows well in aquaponic setups.

Basil: Requires minimal maintenance.

Tomato: High-yielding fruiting plant.

Fish Suitable for Aquaponics

Tilapia: Hardy and easy to grow.

Catfish: Can tolerate varied water conditions.

Goldfish: Ideal for beginners.
