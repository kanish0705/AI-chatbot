What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is an innovative and sustainable agricultural system that combines aquaculture (raising fish) and hydroponics (growing plants without soil) in a mutually beneficial environment. This system allows plants and fish to thrive together, creating a natural, self-sustaining cycle
