Welcome to Sustainable Farming

Aquaponics is an eco-friendly way to grow food by combining fish farming with plant cultivation.

Get Started

What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a **closed-loop system** where fish waste provides nutrients for plants, and plants clean the water.

Best Plants for Aquaponics

Lettuce

Fast-growing and nutrient-efficient.

Basil

Popular for its medicinal uses.

Best Fish for Aquaponics

Tilapia

Hardy and fast-growing.

Goldfish

Best for small-scale systems.

How to Build an Aquaponics System

Follow these steps:

  1. Choose the right location.
  2. Set up a fish tank.
  3. Install a grow bed.
  4. Introduce fish and plants.

How to Earn from Aquaponics

  • Sell organic vegetables.
  • Raise and sell fish.
  • Offer training workshops.
