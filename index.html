What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a combination of aquaculture (raising fish) and hydroponics (growing plants without soil). The fish provide nutrients for the plants, and the plants help clean the water for the fish.

Types of Aquaponics Systems

  • Media Bed System: Uses gravel or clay pebbles.
  • Nutrient Film Technique (NFT): Water flows in shallow channels.
  • Deep Water Culture (DWC): Plants float on rafts in nutrient-rich water.

How to Set Up Your Aquaponics System

  1. Choose a location.
  2. Set up a fish tank and grow beds.
  3. Add pumps, pipes, and aeration.
  4. Introduce fish and plants.
