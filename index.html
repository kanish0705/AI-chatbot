What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is an innovative way to grow plants and fish together. Fish waste provides nutrients for plants, and plants purify the water for the fish.

Why Choose Aquaponics?

  • 🚰 Saves 90% more water than traditional farming
  • 🌿 Grows food organically without pesticides
  • 🐟 Produces fresh fish as a food source
  • 💰 Low maintenance costs after setup

How to Set Up Your Aquaponics System

  1. Choose a fish tank and grow beds
  2. Install water pumps and aeration systems
  3. Add beneficial bacteria to convert waste
  4. Introduce fish and plants
  5. Monitor water quality regularly
