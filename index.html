What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a **self-sustaining farming method** that combines **aquaculture (raising fish)** with **hydroponics (growing plants without soil)**. This system creates a natural cycle where fish waste provides nutrients for plants, and plants clean the water for the fish.

How to Set Up an Aquaponics System

  1. Choose a Location: Ensure **adequate sunlight**.
  2. Fish Tank: Use a **sturdy, food-safe container**.
  3. Grow Bed: Place **plants above the fish tank**.
  4. Install a Water Pump: To circulate nutrients.
  5. Select Fish & Plants: Tilapia, basil, lettuce, and tomatoes work well.
  6. Monitor Water Quality: Maintain **pH, nitrate, and oxygen levels**.
