Setting Up an Aquaponics System

  1. Choose a suitable location (backyard, rooftop, greenhouse).
  2. Install a fish tank and a grow bed.
  3. Use water pumps and aeration systems.
  4. Introduce beneficial bacteria for nutrient conversion.
  5. Add fish and plants after 3-4 weeks.

Common Challenges & Solutions

  • Algae Growth: Reduce sunlight exposure and clean regularly.
  • pH Imbalance: Monitor weekly and adjust with natural buffers.
  • Oxygen Levels: Install aerators or add oxygenating plants.
