What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is a combination of aquaculture (raising fish) and hydroponics (growing plants without soil). It is a sustainable and eco-friendly method for food production.

  • Water Recycling: The fish provide nutrients for plants, and plants clean the water for fish.
  • Minimal Waste: Reduces water usage by up to 90% compared to traditional farming.
  • Organic Growth: No need for chemical fertilizers or pesticides.
